About BookMyShot

BookMyShot is India's premium creator booking platform, connecting talented creators with clients who need their expertise. We make it simple to discover, book, and collaborate with photographers, influencers, YouTubers, singers, models, actors, and event hosts.

Our Mission

To democratize access to creative talent by building a transparent, secure, and efficient marketplace where creators can grow their careers and clients can find the perfect match for their projects.

What We Do

  • Provide a curated marketplace of verified creators across India
  • Enable secure bookings with protected payments
  • Offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Support both creators and clients with dedicated customer service
  • Build tools that help creators manage their business professionally

Our Values

  • Transparency — Clear pricing, honest communication, no surprises
  • Quality — Every creator is vetted before joining the platform
  • Security — Payments are protected, data is encrypted
  • Growth — We invest in tools that help creators succeed
