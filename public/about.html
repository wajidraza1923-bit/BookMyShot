About BookMyShot

India's premium marketplace connecting couples with talented photographers and videographers for life's most memorable moments.

Our Mission

BookMyShot exists to make finding and booking exceptional wedding photographers and videographers effortless. We believe every couple deserves access to verified, talented creators who can capture their special moments with artistry and professionalism.

We bridge the gap between talented creators who deserve visibility and clients who seek quality assurance, transparent pricing, and a seamless booking experience.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for wedding and event photography services — a place where every creator thrives and every client finds their perfect match. We envision a world where no talented photographer goes undiscovered and no couple settles for less than extraordinary.
