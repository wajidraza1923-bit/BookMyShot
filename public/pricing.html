Commission Structure

In addition to the subscription fee, BookMyShot charges a service commission on bookings facilitated through the platform:

  • Platform-sourced bookings: up to 5% commission
  • Creator-sourced bookings: up to 3% commission
Note: Subscription plans, pricing, and features may be updated from time to time. Current pricing is displayed during the creator registration and subscription process. For the latest information, please visit the Creator Dashboard or contact support.
