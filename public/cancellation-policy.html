Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cancellation Policy governs the terms under which bookings made through BookMyShot may be cancelled by either Clients or Creators. We encourage both parties to honour confirmed bookings.

1. Cancellation by Client

Clients may cancel a confirmed booking subject to the following timeline:

Cancellation Timeline Refund Amount Penalty
30+ days before event Full refund (minus convenience fee) None
15-30 days before event 75% refund 25% retained by Creator
7-14 days before event 50% refund 50% retained by Creator
3-6 days before event 25% refund 75% retained by Creator
Less than 3 days No refund Full amount retained by Creator

2. Cancellation by Creator

Creators are expected to honour all confirmed bookings. However, if a Creator must cancel:

Cancellation Timeline Client Refund Creator Penalty
30+ days before event Full refund to Client Warning issued
15-30 days before event Full refund to Client Profile visibility reduced for 7 days
7-14 days before event Full refund + 10% compensation Profile visibility reduced for 15 days
Less than 7 days Full refund + 20% compensation Account suspension risk + review

Note: Repeated cancellations by a Creator (3 or more in 6 months) may result in permanent account suspension.

3. Mutual Cancellation

If both Client and Creator mutually agree to cancel a booking:

  • Full refund is issued to the Client
  • No penalty is applied to the Creator
  • Both parties must confirm the mutual cancellation through the platform
  • Platform commission is refunded

4. Force Majeure

In cases of unforeseen events beyond either party's control (natural disasters, government restrictions, pandemics, etc.):

  • Full refund is issued to the Client
  • No penalty is applied to either party
  • BookMyShot will assist in rescheduling where possible
  • Proof of the force majeure event may be required

5. No-Show Policy

  • Client no-show: If the Client does not show up for the scheduled event without prior cancellation, no refund will be issued
  • Creator no-show: If the Creator fails to appear without prior notice, the Client receives a full refund plus 25% compensation. The Creator's account will be reviewed for suspension

6. Rescheduling

Instead of cancelling, both parties may agree to reschedule:

  • Rescheduling is free if done 15+ days before the event
  • A rescheduling fee of 10% may apply if done within 7-14 days
  • Rescheduling within 7 days is subject to Creator availability and may incur additional charges
  • Each booking may be rescheduled a maximum of 2 times

7. How to Cancel

  1. Log in to your BookMyShot account
  2. Go to "My Bookings" section
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking" and provide a reason
  5. Confirm the cancellation

For assistance, contact us at bookmyshott@gmail.com
