How BookMyShot Works

BookMyShot is India's premium platform connecting couples with verified wedding photographers, videographers, and cinematographers.

What is BookMyShot

BookMyShot is an online marketplace that helps users discover, compare, and book professional wedding creators. We provide creators with tools to manage their business, showcase their portfolio, and receive bookings through our platform.

For Users

  • Browse verified creator profiles and portfolios
  • Compare pricing, packages, and reviews
  • Send inquiries and book creators directly
  • Track bookings and communicate with creators
  • Submit payment proofs for booking confirmation

For Creators

  • Creators can create accounts and submit profiles for approval
  • Approved creators get access to the Creator Dashboard
  • Manage portfolio, packages, bookings, and inquiries
  • Receive leads and booking requests from users
  • Track earnings, commissions, and payment history
  • Access promotional tools for enhanced visibility

Creator Verification

All creator accounts undergo a manual review process by our team before activation. This ensures quality, authenticity, and safety for users booking through our platform. Only verified and approved creators appear in public listings.

Subscription Plans

BookMyShot may charge monthly creator subscription fees for premium dashboard features.

Subscription plans provide creators with access to the full dashboard, portfolio management, booking tools, analytics, and visibility features. Plan pricing is configured by the platform administrator and may be updated from time to time.

  • Free Trial may be available for new creators
  • Monthly subscription required for continued access after trial
  • Subscription pricing is displayed during the creator registration process

Platform Commission

BookMyShot may charge up to 5% commission on successful bookings and leads generated through the platform.

  • Commission is calculated on the total booking amount
  • BookMyShot-sourced leads: up to 5% commission
  • Creator-sourced leads: up to 3% commission
  • Commission percentage is configured by the platform and may be updated
  • Commission is stored permanently on each booking at the time of creation

How BookMyShot Earns Revenue

BookMyShot generates revenue through the following sources:

  • Creator Subscriptions: Monthly subscription fees for dashboard access and premium features
  • Booking Commissions: Percentage-based commission on successful bookings facilitated through the platform
  • Featured Listings: Paid featured placement on homepage and search results
  • Premium Promotions: Rank-based promotion slots for enhanced creator visibility
  • Advertising: Promotional services and spotlight placements

Refund Policy

Creator subscription fees are generally non-refundable once the subscription period has been activated. For booking-related refunds, please refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy page.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a photographer?+

Browse our creators, view their portfolio and packages, then send an inquiry or book directly from their profile page. The creator will accept your request and schedule the event.

Are creators verified?+

Yes, all creators go through a manual verification process before appearing on the platform. We verify their identity, portfolio, and professional background.

What is the subscription for creators?+

Creators pay a monthly subscription to access the dashboard, manage bookings, showcase their portfolio, and receive leads. A free trial may be available for new creators.

How does BookMyShot earn money?+

Through creator subscriptions, booking commissions (up to 5%), featured listings, and premium promotion slots. See the Revenue section above for details.

Can I cancel my booking?+

Please refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy for detailed information on booking cancellations and refund eligibility.

Is my payment secure?+

Yes. All payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card details on our servers.
