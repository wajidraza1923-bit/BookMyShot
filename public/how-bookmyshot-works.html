How BookMyShot Works

A simple step-by-step guide for users and creators

For Users

Book Your Dream Creator in 4 Steps

1. Browse & Discover

Explore verified photographers and videographers. Filter by city, budget, style, and rating. View portfolios and real work.

2. Send an Enquiry

Found someone you like? Submit an enquiry with your event details — date, location, budget, and requirements. No login required.

3. Get Matched & Confirm

The creator reviews your request and accepts. You'll discuss details, finalize the package, and confirm the booking.

4. Event Day & Delivery

Creator arrives at your event, captures every moment. You receive your photos/videos as per the agreed timeline.

For Creators

Grow Your Business in 5 Steps

1. Apply & Get Verified

Submit your application with portfolio samples. Our team reviews and approves quality creators within 24–48 hours.

2. Set Up Your Profile

Upload portfolio photos/videos, set your packages, pricing, availability calendar, and service areas.

3. Receive Bookings & Leads

Get enquiries from users browsing the platform. Accept or negotiate — you're in full control.

4. Manage Everything

Track bookings, payments, calendar events, and client communication — all from your Creator Dashboard.

5. Get Paid & Grow

Receive payments directly. Build your reputation with reviews. Boost visibility with promotions.
