Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Client Cancellations

  • Before creator accepts: Free cancellation, full refund
  • After acceptance, 7+ days before event: 80% refund
  • 3-7 days before event: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days before event: No refund

Creator Cancellations

  • Creators may cancel a booking at any time before work begins
  • Clients receive a full refund when creators cancel
  • Repeated cancellations may affect the creator's platform standing
  • Creator accounts with excessive cancellations may be reviewed or suspended

How to Cancel

  1. Go to your booking dashboard
  2. Find the booking you wish to cancel
  3. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm
  4. Refund (if applicable) will be processed automatically

Exceptions

In cases of emergency or force majeure (natural disasters, medical emergencies), please contact support directly. We handle these on a case-by-case basis with full consideration of circumstances.

Contact
