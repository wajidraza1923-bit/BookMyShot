Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BookMyShot

Account & Getting Started
How do I create an account on BookMyShot?

Click "Login" or "Register" from the homepage. Enter your name, email, and password. You'll receive a verification OTP on your email. Once verified, your account is ready to use.

What's the difference between a User and a Creator account?

Users browse creators and submit booking enquiries. Creators are professional photographers/videographers who manage portfolios, accept bookings, and provide services. Creators need admin approval to appear on the platform.

How do I become a creator on BookMyShot?

Click "Apply as Creator" from the homepage menu. Fill in your details, specialty, and portfolio samples. Our team reviews applications within 24–48 hours. Once approved, you get access to the Creator Dashboard.

Bookings & Enquiries
How do I book a photographer?

Browse creators on the platform, view their portfolio and packages, then submit an enquiry with your event details. The creator will accept and confirm your booking. No login is required to submit an enquiry.

Can I book without creating an account?

Yes! You can submit a general enquiry from the homepage without logging in. Our team will match you with the right creator. For advanced features like messaging and booking tracking, you'll need an account.

How long does a creator take to respond?

Most creators respond within 2–24 hours. You'll receive a notification when the creator accepts or responds to your enquiry.

Can I cancel a booking?

Cancellation policies vary by creator and booking stage. Please refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy for full details. Generally, early cancellations have better refund eligibility.

Payments & Pricing
Is BookMyShot free for users?

Yes! Browsing creators, viewing portfolios, and submitting enquiries is completely free for users. You only pay the creator directly for their services.

How do payments work?

Payments are made directly between you and the creator. BookMyShot provides payment tracking within the booking. Creators can record advance, partial, and final payments in the system.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All online payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card or bank details on our servers.

What is the platform commission?

BookMyShot charges creators a small commission (3–5%) on bookings facilitated through the platform. This is deducted from the creator's earnings, not from the user's payment.

For Creators
How much does a creator subscription cost?

Creator subscription pricing is set by the platform and may include a free trial period. Current pricing is displayed during the registration process. The subscription gives access to the full dashboard, booking tools, and lead generation.

What happens after my trial expires?

After your trial ends, Razorpay AutoPay will automatically charge the monthly subscription fee. If payment fails, your account features will be restricted until payment is resolved.

How do I get more bookings?

Complete your profile with quality portfolio images, competitive pricing, and detailed packages. You can also use BookMyShot's promotion features (Featured listings, Rank promotions) to increase your visibility.

Are creators verified?

Yes. Every creator application goes through a manual review process. We verify identity, portfolio quality, and professional background before approving any creator on the platform.

Support & Policies
How do I contact BookMyShot support?

Email us at support@bookmyshot.in or use the Contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What is BookMyShot's refund policy?

Creator subscription fees are generally non-refundable. For booking-related refunds, eligibility depends on the cancellation timeline and creator's policy. See our Refund Policy for full details.
