Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At BookMyShot, we strive to provide a fair and transparent refund process for all users. This policy outlines the terms for refunds across our different service categories.

Important: All refund requests must be submitted within the specified timelines. Requests received after the deadline may not be eligible for processing.

1. Subscription Refunds (Creator Plans)

BookMyShot offers subscription plans for creators to access premium features:

  • Within 48 hours of purchase: Full refund available if no premium features have been used
  • After 48 hours: No refund for the current billing period
  • Auto-renewal charges: Refundable within 24 hours of the renewal charge if you forgot to cancel
  • Plan downgrades: No prorated refunds; the current plan remains active until the billing cycle ends

To cancel your subscription, go to your account settings or contact our support team.

2. Commission Refunds

BookMyShot charges a platform commission on bookings facilitated through the platform:

  • Booking cancelled by Client: Commission is refunded to the Creator if the Client cancels within the eligible cancellation window
  • Booking cancelled by Creator: Commission is refunded to the Client; Creator may be penalized
  • Mutual cancellation: Commission is refunded to both parties
  • Completed bookings: Commission is non-refundable once the service has been delivered
  • Disputed bookings: Commission refund is determined after dispute resolution

3. Promotion & Featured Listing Refunds

Creators who purchase promotional placements or featured listings:

  • Before promotion goes live: Full refund available
  • Within first 24 hours of going live: 50% refund if the promotion has not generated significant impressions
  • After 24 hours of going live: No refund available
  • Technical issues: If our platform error prevented the promotion from displaying correctly, a full refund or credit will be issued

4. Booking Payment Refunds

For payments made through the BookMyShot platform for booking services:

  • Advance/token amount: Refund eligibility depends on cancellation timing (see Cancellation Policy)
  • Full payment: Refund as per the cancellation timeline agreed during booking
  • Service not delivered: Full refund if the Creator fails to deliver the agreed service
  • Partial service delivery: Prorated refund based on the extent of service delivered, at BookMyShot's discretion

5. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method
  • Bank processing time may add an additional 3-5 business days
  • UPI refunds are typically faster (1-3 business days)

6. Non-Refundable Items

  • Convenience fees and payment gateway charges
  • Services that have been fully delivered and accepted
  • Subscriptions used beyond the 48-hour window
  • Promotional credits and discount coupons

7. How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your BookMyShot account
  2. Navigate to your booking/subscription history
  3. Select the transaction and click "Request Refund"
  4. Provide the reason for your refund request
  5. Our team will review and respond within 48 hours

Alternatively, email us at bookmyshott@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the refund.

8. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may escalate the matter by contacting our support team. BookMyShot's decision in dispute resolution is final and binding.
