Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

When Refunds Apply

  • Creator cancels the booking before work begins — full refund
  • Creator fails to deliver agreed work within the deadline — full or partial refund
  • Client cancels before creator accepts — full refund
  • Client cancels after creator accepts — partial refund (see table below)
  • Platform error causing double charge — full refund of duplicate

Refund Schedule

Cancellation TimingRefund Amount
Before creator accepts100% refund
After acceptance, 7+ days before event80% refund
After acceptance, 3-7 days before event50% refund
After acceptance, less than 3 daysNo refund
Creator cancels (any time)100% refund

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. Bank processing may take an additional 2-3 days.

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our support team at support@bookmyshot.in
  • Include your booking ID and reason for refund
  • Our team will review and process within 48 hours

Non-Refundable Items

  • Platform subscription fees (creator subscriptions)
  • Completed bookings where work was delivered as agreed
  • Bookings cancelled less than 3 days before the event

Creator Subscription Refunds

Creator subscription fees are non-refundable once the subscription period has been activated and the creator has been granted access to the dashboard. Subscriptions are billed on a monthly basis. If you believe you were charged in error, please contact our support team within 7 days of the transaction. For more details about subscription plans, visit How BookMyShot Works.
