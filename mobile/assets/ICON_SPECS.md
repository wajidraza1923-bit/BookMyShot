# BookMyShot App Icon Specifications

## Design Concept
- **Background:** Solid black (#0A0A0A)
- **Primary element:** Gold camera shutter/lens ring (#D4AF37)
- **Center:** Stylish "B" monogram in gold
- **No text** — just the icon symbol
- **Style:** Premium, luxury, modern

## Required Files (place in mobile/assets/)

### 1. icon.png (1024×1024px)
- Full app icon with background
- Used for iOS and general purpose
- Black background, gold camera shutter with B

### 2. android-icon-foreground.png (1024×1024px)
- Just the camera shutter + B element
- Transparent background
- Content should be within the inner 66% (safe zone for adaptive icons)
- This goes on TOP of the background

### 3. android-icon-background.png (1024×1024px)
- Solid black (#0A0A0A) background
- Can be just a plain black 1024x1024 PNG

### 4. splash-icon.png (512×512px or 1024×1024px)
- The BookMyShot logo for splash screen
- Gold camera shutter with B on transparent or black background
- Centered, clean

### 5. notification-icon.png (96×96px)
- White/monochrome version of the icon
- For Android notification tray (must be single color)
